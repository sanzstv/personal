Who is...Stevie Sanz?

I am a design-oriented software engineer who excels at combining both the aesthetic and the functional aspects of web design.

I graduated from Santa Clara University in 2016 with a Bachelor's in Computer Science & Engineering and a minor in Studio Art. Since then I have worked at an eCommerce startup where I supplemented my technical abilities with my design eye in order to create beautiful widgets for external clients and internal projects.

I absolutely love learning new skills and finding new ways to solve problems.

A (much needed) redesign I did of one of the internet's most popular websites. This redesign was crafted to avoid the pitfalls that befell Reddit's predecessor Digg after they overhauled their website. Emphasis on maintaining current site's emphasis on information density while also making much more aesthetically pleasing and functional. Just something I did for a class and unfortunately has not been implemented, but if anyone from Reddit has a change of heart and decides "yeah, my website is massively flawed, maybe we should do something about it", just let me know!
